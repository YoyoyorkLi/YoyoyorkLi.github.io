### Overview
Using the Meet Pandora smartphone app for daily emoji-based affect tracking, this study demonstrated that the proportion of positive emojis used is a significant predictor of lower symptom burden and higher psychological flourishing in college students.
<br><br>
### My Role: First Author & Lead Data Scientist

* **Lead Authorship:** Authored the majority of the manuscript, translating complex statistical findings into an academic paper for publication.

* **Data Wrangling & Cleaning:** Cleaned and joined multimodal datasets using Python, identifying and filtering biomarker outliers.

* **Statistical Modeling:** Built and evaluated mixed-effects regression models in R to isolate the predictive power of vocal biomarkers while controlling for baseline individual differences.
